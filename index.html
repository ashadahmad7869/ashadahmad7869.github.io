About me...

Hi! I am Ashad Ahmad. I am currently a final year graduate student pursuing Masters in Astronomy from Indian Institute of Technology Indore.

I am currently studying the interaction between the relativistic AGN jets and the magnetic field present in the Intra-Cluster Medium (ICM) in galaxy clusters and various galaxy mergers. Cold fronts arising due to collision between cold subcluster cores of one cluster and the surrounding hot gas. These cold fronts have contact discontinuity changing various properties of the gas along with magnetic field with gets draped around these cold front. AGN jets, on interacting with these fields produce peculiar shapes. I perform simulation using PLUTO Code of these interaction and study the resulting morphology along with their spectral maps.
